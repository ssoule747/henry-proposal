The Challenge

Annual reviews shouldn't take weeks to produce.

Every year, your team opens last year's Keynote for each client, manually updates financial figures, rewrites market commentary, adjusts dates and projections, and reformats charts. It's skilled work done by skilled people — but it's repetitive.

Multiply that across your entire client base and you're looking at weeks of time spent on mechanical edits instead of the strategic advising your clients actually value.

Meet Henry

Your AI Presentation Assistant

Henry is a private AI assistant that lives on your Mac Mini. Drop in last year's Keynote alongside new financial data — portfolio statements, tax summaries, CSV exports — and tell Henry what to change in plain English. He reads everything, cross-references the numbers, and rebuilds the deck in minutes.

Henry runs entirely on your local network. No client data ever leaves your office.

How It Works

Four steps. One conversation.

1
Drop in Your Files
Last year's Keynote, new portfolio statements, tax summaries — anything Henry needs to build the update.
2
Tell Henry What to Change
Type in plain English: “Update the financials to 2025 actuals and make the commentary more conservative.” Henry understands context.
3
Review Every Change
Henry shows you each slide with a detailed changelog. Click any slide to see exactly what was modified and why.
4
Present to Your Client
Download the finished Keynote. Open it directly, review the slides, and walk into your next meeting prepared.

The Opportunity Cost

Every hour your advisors spend reformatting slides is an hour they’re not spending with clients. In wealth management, relationships are the product. The firms that grow aren’t the ones with the best presentations — they’re the ones whose advisors have time to actually sit with people.

Henry doesn’t replace your team’s expertise. He handles the mechanical work — updating numbers, rewriting dates, adjusting projections — so your people can focus on what they were hired to do: advise.

And the math compounds. The 93 hours you reclaim this year don’t just save money once. Those hours become client conversations that generate recurring AUM fees for years. A single new client relationship started in reclaimed time could generate $50,000+ in revenue over a decade.

The Roadmap

Henry grows with your practice. 10 planned capabilities

Phase 1 handles presentations. But Henry's architecture is built to become the AI backbone of your entire client workflow.

⚡
Batch Processing
▾

Feed Henry a folder of 50 client data files. Wake up to 50 updated presentations. Transform a multi-week project into an overnight task.

Phase 2
👤
Client Profiles
▾

Henry remembers each client's preferences — “The Hendersons prefer conservative language,” “The Chens want detail on international exposure,” “Mr. Park needs large print.” Every deck is automatically personalized.

Phase 2
📋
Meeting Prep Briefs
▾

Alongside each deck, Henry generates a one-page brief: key changes to highlight, questions the client might ask, and suggested talking points for the meeting.

Phase 2
✉
Post-Meeting Follow-Ups
▾

After the meeting, tell Henry what was discussed. He drafts a personalized follow-up email to the client, referencing specific topics from the conversation.

Phase 2
📊
Quarterly Mini-Decks
▾

Not just annual reviews — Henry generates lighter quarterly check-in presentations from your latest portfolio data exports.

Phase 2
🔗
Portfolio Software Integration
▾

Direct connection to Orion, Black Diamond, Tamarac, or your portfolio management system. Henry pulls data automatically — no more CSV uploads.

Phase 3
🛡
Compliance Pre-Check
▾

Henry flags language that might conflict with SEC/FINRA guidelines before the deck reaches a client — forward-looking statements, performance claims, missing disclaimers.

Phase 2
🌐
Client Presentation Portal
▾

A branded web portal where clients log in to view their presentations online. No more emailing large files — everything is secure, trackable, and professional.

Phase 3
🎨
Brand Consistency Engine
▾

Henry enforces Crew Wealth's brand guidelines across every deck — fonts, colors, logo placement, disclaimer language. Nothing goes out off-brand.

Phase 2
🎤
Voice Instructions
▾

Dictate changes to Henry from your phone: “Hey Henry, update the Henderson deck with their Q3 numbers and add a slide about the Roth conversion.” Done.

Phase 3

Every capability builds on the same foundation. Phase 1 creates the core — each addition after that is incremental, not a rebuild.

Technical Approach

Built for privacy and simplicity.

Runs on Your Mac Mini
Henry operates entirely on your local machine. Client financial data never touches the cloud, never leaves your office network. The AI processes everything on-device through a secure API connection.
Anthropic's Claude AI
Henry is built on Claude, the most capable AI for document analysis and writing. Claude reads financial data, understands context, and writes with the nuance and accuracy your clients expect.
Native Apple Integration
Henry talks directly to Keynote through Apple's scripting layer. Your themes, transitions, master slides, and brand assets are preserved perfectly — the output is a real .key file, not a conversion.
