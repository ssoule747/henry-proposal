These aren’t failures — they’re the natural ceiling of a practice that runs on human memory and manual processes. Your team is exceptional at the work that matters. They’re just spending too much of their time on the work that doesn’t.

TECHNICAL APPROACH

Built for privacy, built to last.

Private & Local

Everything runs on your Mac Mini. Client financial data never leaves your office. No cloud storage, no third-party servers. The intelligence layer processes data locally through a secure API connection to Anthropic’s Claude — the data goes out, the analysis comes back, nothing is stored.

Anthropic’s Claude AI

The intelligence layer is built on Claude, the most capable AI for document analysis, financial reasoning, and nuanced writing. Claude doesn’t just find-and-replace — it understands context, catches relationships between data points, and writes with the professionalism your clients expect.

Works With Your Tools

Henry communicates with Keynote natively through Apple’s scripting layer. The intelligence layer is designed to connect to standard financial software APIs, CRM systems, and file storage. It adapts to your existing stack — you don’t adapt to it.
